​Dynamic packaging for Ice Cream

The grow carton was designed to allow Ice cream to expand within the package to thwart the container lid from popping off during transit causing the product to spill out during transit. The packaging problem represents a huge loss to the maker and also creates a risk to the sanitation and safety of the packages content. With the patent pending “grow carton” the container can expand when shipped to higher elevations without compromising the content. The accordion style pleats that at first are canceled under the deep lid cuff, can deploy to accommodate the expanding Frozen content.
